Who We Are

About FBTC

Established in 2011, Future Building Training Consultants has grown into a trusted, nationally-recognised accredited training and medical services provider.

Our Vision

Train for a Better Tomorrow

With the ever-changing and challenging South African economy, our vision is to give learners the required skills to be employable. The return on investment for our clients is a skilled, legislatively compliant workforce.

Our Mission

Market Leadership Through Quality

We aim to become a market leader within our scope of accreditation, providing portable skills for the South African workforce through quality, accredited training interventions that produce measurable results.

Our Story

Founded 2011 by Francois Gantz

FBT Consultants (Reg. No. 2011/031684/23) was established in September 2011 with a focus on Training and Development Solutions. Built on strong moral principles, we've grown into a proud company serving clients across South Africa.

Our primary accreditation is with the Transport Education and Training Authority (TETA) — Registration No. TETA12-223. We are additionally registered with CETA, SAIOSH, the Department of Labour, and the Department of Transport.

Our Quality Management System is managed by our managing member, ensuring clients receive the support they require. All learner data is captured onto our customised Learner Management System — enabling proactive renewal management before compliance issues arise.

We hold a Level 4 B-BBEE certificate with 100% procurement recognition, and an occupational health nurse is on standby for operator and full medicals (Western Cape only).
